In Four simple steps

Drawing a symetrical face can be tricky for those who didn't grow up obsessing over art, comics or cartoons.

  1. Draw the head shape ![Step 1](http://www.wikihow.com/Draw-a-Face#/Image:Draw-a-Face-Step-1-Version-3.jpg)
  2. Lightly>/i> draw an axis across the face.![Step 2](http://www.wikihow.com/Draw-a-Face#/Image:Draw-a-Face-Step-2-Version-3.jpg)
  3. Add a nose, eyes and mouth across the axis lines.![Step 3](http://www.wikihow.com/Draw-a-Face#/Image:Draw-a-Face-Step-5Bullet3.jpg)
  4. Erase the axis lines, add further details, hair if you want.![Step 4](http://www.wikihow.com/Draw-a-Face#/Image:Draw-a-Face-Step-12-Version-2.jpg)
